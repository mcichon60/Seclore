Days 31–60 · Activation

Outreach, content, and partner co-sell live

  • Launch AI-supported SDR sequences for the top 50 North America target accounts — persona-specific for CISO, data governance, compliance, and CIO buyers with ARMOR proof points from banking (1.5M+ PII docs), healthcare (10,000+ files/month), and semiconductor use cases
  • Publish ARMOR pillar page (2,500+ words), "What is Data Security Intelligence" guide, and "EDRM vs. DLP vs. DSPM" comparison with FAQ schema and HowTo markup
